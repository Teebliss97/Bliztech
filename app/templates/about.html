About BlizTech

Helping people stay safe online through clear, practical cybersecurity education.

Why BlizTech exists

What BlizTech offers

  • Clear, jargon-free cybersecurity lessons

Who BlizTech is for

BlizTech is designed for:

  • Students learning about cybersecurity
  • Professionals improving digital safety awareness
  • Small business owners and entrepreneurs
  • Everyday internet users who want to stay safe online

About the founder

BlizTech was created by Toheeb Adebayo Atinuke, a cybersecurity professional and digital technologies educator with an MSc in Cybersecurity.

With experience in cybersecurity awareness, online safety education, and practical security principles, Toheeb built BlizTech to bridge the gap between technical cybersecurity knowledge and everyday users.

The goal is simple: empower people with the knowledge they need to protect themselves online.
