Online Shopping & Payment Safety

Shopping scams are common: fake stores, fake delivery texts, and payment tricks. This topic helps you avoid losing money.

How to shop safely

{{ bullets("Safe shopping habits", [ "Buy from reputable websites and verified sellers", "Check reviews (not only on the site itself)", "Be cautious with deals that are ‘too cheap’", "Use card payment where possible (better protection than transfer)", "Never share OTP, PIN, or full card details with strangers" ]) }}

Delivery scam signs

  • “Pay a small fee to reschedule delivery” from unknown sender
  • Link goes to a suspicious domain
  • Message creates urgency
