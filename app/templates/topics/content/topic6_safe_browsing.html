Safe Browsing & Downloads

Many attacks happen while browsing — fake sites, fake buttons, and downloads that look legit. This topic teaches you how to click safely.

Red flags

  • Pop-ups saying “Your device is infected”
  • Forced downloads without you clicking
  • Sites asking you to install extensions to view content
